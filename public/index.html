How to play:

You've set up a picnic at the local park, but soon bugs start trying to overrun your food! Use your bug-swatter to keep them at bay, and try to hold them off for as long as possible!
  • Use your mouse to move the bug-swatter around the page, clicking on bugs to smoosh them.
  • You lose a health point for each bug that makes it onto your picnic.
  • The game ends when you lose all of your health.
